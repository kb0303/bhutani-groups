ABOUT CITY CENTER

Bhutani City Centre 150 Noida is a glimpse into the future lifestyle and retail areas in Delhi NCR. With al-fresco style high-street retail in a low-rise development, the project is built to immerse visitors in a world of seamless convenience.

City Center 150 announces itself in the grandest way possible, with a 500+ feet (158 metres) frontage and a modern glass façade with graphical clean lines forming interesting patterns. Located on the banks of a 30 m wide road, the design of Bhutani City Center 150 invites visitors to indulge in retail therapy while providing brands with glittering retail spaces in Nodia to stake their claim in the hearts and minds of the consumers. And if that doesn’t take your breath away, the park views that greet diners at the many F&B outlets spread across the food court space in Noida will offer you another excuse to halt and take it all in.
