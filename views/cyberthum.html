ABOUT CYBERTHUM

Welcome to India’s one of the most futuristic commercial real estate complex-The Bhutani Cyberthum noida sector 140. It is situated at a prime business location in Sector-140A, Noida. The respective site location of the Bhutani Cyberthum is connected with the Delhi NCR through newly built metro stations in sector 142. Besides that, one can also access it through FNG and Noida Expressway, which provide additional strength to this commercial location in terms of super-fast connectivity and seamless public transportation.

The project was launched by the Bhutani Group, especially after having a grand financial success in the Bhutani Alphathum. The Bhutani Infra Cyberthum Sector 140A Noida is spread across 26.8 acres of picturesque landscapes and includes two magnificent towers rising up to 50 stories. The project offers fully furnished office spaces and retail stores with a 12 % assured return on Investment. With its 20 plus public amenities and modern features, the project provides an outstanding work environment for businesses, brands and startups.

FAQ's

Bhutani Cyberthum is a commercial project by Bhutani Group in Sector 140A, Noida.

Cyberthum by Bhutani Infra is located at Sector 140A, Noida Expressway.

Being a commercial project, Bhutani Cyberthum noida has office, retail shops and high-street retail stores. It also has a sky mall proposed as part of the commercial project.

Bhutani Group's Cyberthum is spread over 26.8 Acres of mixed development land allocated by the Noida Authority.

Bhutani Group is known for its past delivered project iThum in Noida. Its other prominent projects are Alphathum in Sector 90 Noida, Cyberthum in Sector 140A Noida & Grandthum in Noida Extension.

Bhutani Group's Cyberthum has received great response and reviews from both commercial real estate investors and end-users. It offers exciting opportunity for investment as well as setting up own office space.
